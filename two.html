App Proposal Video for USC Iovine and Young Academy

After submitting a portfolio for the USC IYA, a program founded by entrepreneurs Jimmy Iovine and Andre Young (Dr. Dre), I was one of the 70 students selected out of hundreds to be flown to California for interviews. Having an acceptance rate around 5%, I was honored for the opportunity. I was not selected for the program after interviews, but enjoyed creating art pieces for the portfolio and met some incredible entrepreneurs that weekend.
