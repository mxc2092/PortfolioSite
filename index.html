Education

As a member of the program I took a technology course each year of high school, took part in workshops better preparing me for the technology industry, and completed a 200+ hour internship. I also took part in my school's signature Arts and Hummanities program in which I was surrounded by other students passionate about hummanities, placed in more interactive class environments, and completed a research project on the topic of my choice. I chose to research digital privacy and the implications of aggreeing to terms and conditions in our technology-centered world. View my Research Paper!

I am now pursing my bachelor of science in Interactive Media at the University of Miami in Coral Gables, Florida. I am a member of the Communications honors program where I am surrounded with other students passionate in comunications as well as get flexibility in building my own academic path.
