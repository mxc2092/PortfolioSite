Hi, I am Margaret Christovich. I am a first year at the University of Miami in Coral Gables, Florida. I created this site entirely from scratch to showcase some of my work and practice working with web languages.

I am a member of the Communications Honors Program at the U where I am building my own academic path. Currently, I am taking courses centered in website design, computer science, animation, interactive media, and graphic design.

In high school, I took part in a few signature programs that sparked my interest in design and technology. I completed the Academy of Information Technology following the Website Design and Development Pathway. As a member of the program, I took a technology course each year of high school, completed workshops better preparing me for the technology industry, and completed a 200+ hour internship.

I also took part in my school's signature Arts and Humanities program in which I completed a research project on the topic of my choice. I chose to research digital privacy and the implications of agreeing to terms and conditions in our technology-centered world. View my Research Paper!

Aside from these programs, I enjoyed being involved with field hockey, track, lacrosse, and a few honors societies.
